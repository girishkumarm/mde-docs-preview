## Blocker summary (must fix before full cutover)

1. **ClickHouse TTL conflict** — V3 migration overrides v2's 180-day target with 7 years on candles_1m. Decide and align.
2. **JWT enforcement missing on 10 of 11 services** — only instrument-master validates Keycloak JWT. Remaining /v1/** endpoints are open.
3. **No Jacoco coverage gate** — CI does not fail on coverage regressions.
4. **No circuit breakers** — CMOTS, ClickHouse and Redis calls have no Resilience4j wiring despite plan references.
5. **Canary + rollback plan undocumented** — source=auto flag exists but traffic-shift and roll-back steps are not written.
6. **pm-jobs-sdk on mavenLocal** — real CodeArtifact release required.
7. **Oncall + dashboard URLs** — runbook annotations reference https://wiki/mde/runbooks/* placeholder, no paging rotation published.

## Ready-for-canary summary

Code, observability scaffolding (scrape + dashboards + alert rules), graceful shutdown, per-service Kafka consumer groups, k6 bake-off script, source=auto feature flag, and 6 runbooks are in place. Safe to run a time-boxed canary behind source=auto while the seven blockers above are closed out.
