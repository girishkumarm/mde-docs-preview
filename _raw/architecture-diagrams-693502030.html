Market Data Engine — Architecture Diagrams

This page contains all architecture diagrams for the Market Data Engine design document. Each diagram is stored as an .excalidraw file (open in excalidraw.com to view/edit) and also rendered as SVG below.

v3 diagrams are the current architecture. The v2 diagrams at the bottom are kept for historical reference only.
